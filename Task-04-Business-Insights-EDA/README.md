# Task 04 - Business Insights Report using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objective

The objective of this project is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Marketing Campaign dataset to identify customer behavior, purchasing patterns, campaign effectiveness, and business opportunities. The analysis helps organizations make data-driven marketing decisions and optimize future marketing strategies.

---

## Tools Used

- Python
- Google Colab
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib

---

## Dataset

Dataset Name: Marketing Campaign Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographic information, purchasing history, campaign responses, income details, education, marital status, and product spending.

---

## Data Cleaning

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

- Imported the dataset into Google Colab.
- Loaded the dataset using the correct delimiter.
- Checked data types.
- Identified missing values.
- Filled missing Income values using the median.
- Checked duplicate records.
- Verified the cleaned dataset.

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis

The following visualizations were created:

- Customer Distribution by Education
- Income Distribution
- Marketing Campaign Response
- Product Spending Analysis
- Purchase Channel Analysis
- Customer Age Distribution
- Correlation Matrix
- Marketing Funnel
- Average Income by Education

---

## Business Insights

- Most customers belong to higher education categories.
- Customer income varies significantly across education levels.
- A small percentage of customers accepted the marketing campaign.
- Wine products generated the highest customer spending.
- Store purchases were higher than web and catalog purchases.
- Customers mainly belong to the middle-age category.
- Income has a positive relationship with product spending.
- Marketing campaigns can be optimized by targeting high-income customer segments.
